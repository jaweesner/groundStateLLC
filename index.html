Who We Are

Forrest Weesner is an independent chemical instrumentation consultant offering applications support, method development, and training on vibrational spectroscopy techniques. Forrest also supports marketing teams of chemical analysis companies with technical marketing content and general marketing strategies. Forrest has 30 years of experience developing and instrumentation and analysis methods for academic, government and commercial organizations. He is an expert user of the entire Thermo Scientific vibrational spectroscopy product line having held several positions in applications, product development, and product management position at the company. Additional experience in separation science, elemental analysis, and laboratory quality assurance completes a broad technical backdrop that informs Forrest’s practical, results oriented approach to his consulting projects.
